About game
This is an easy-to-play game that could become your next source of unlimited fun.

It may be difficult and very challenging at times but it would surely take away boredom.

If you think you know about colors or you want to learn more about color combinations then this is the game to play.

It is very educational and good for kids that are currently learning colors in art class, it is also good for art students and graphic designers when looking for color combinations.

With a simple yet stellar UI and eye-catching features, you are guaranteed a fun experience while playing Color Match.
